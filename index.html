Folk Stringed Instruments

These stringed instruments are commonly played by folk singers around the world.

Music Transcriber

Guitar

The acoustic guitar is a staple of folk music, providing rhythmic accompaniment and melodic lines.

Banjo

Known for its bright, percussive sound, the banjo adds driving rhythms to folk and bluegrass tunes.

Mandolin

With its crisp tremolo, the mandolin brings a shimmering texture to many folk melodies.

Fiddle

Essential in many traditions, the fiddle carries lively reels and heartfelt laments.

Dulcimer

The Appalachian mountain dulcimer offers a sweet, droning sound ideal for ballads.
